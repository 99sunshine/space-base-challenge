01

Cross-Disciplinary by Design

跨学科，集大成

Most hackathons are built for engineers. Ours has interdisciplinary tracks — Engineering, Governance, Aesthetics, and Open Innovation. With AI lowering technical barriers, political scientists, designers, writers, and artists are no longer spectators. They're co-creators.

传统黑客松几乎只属于工程师。我们从一开始就设立了四条并行赛道——工程技术、社会治理、空间美学、开放创新。当 AI 正在消解技术壁垒，哲学家、建筑师、编剧和艺术家终于可以站到舞台中央，与工程师共创未来蓝图。

02

Imagination-Driven, Not Application-Driven

想象力驱动，而非复制已有的应用

We don't ask students to clone the next app. We ask them to imagine humanity's next home. The prompt is a civilizational question — how should we live, govern, and create in space? This produces work with real intellectual depth, not just technical demos.

我们不让参赛者做下一个外卖App或社交工具。我们向他们抛出的是文明尺度的追问：人类该如何在太空中居住、治理与创造？当命题足够宏大，产出才会有真正的思想重量。这不只是一个技术Demo。

03

International & Cross-Cultural

跨越太平洋的创造碰撞

Simultaneous nodes in international cities: New York and Beijing. Teams can communicate across borders. Daily "Stellar Link" sessions connect all sites in real-time. One of the first hackathons to bridge top American and Chinese universities around a shared creative mission.

北京与纽约同步开赛，选手可以跨国交流。每天的「星际链接」环节将所有节点实时连线。这是首批将中美顶尖高校围绕同一创作使命连接在一起的黑客松——我们相信，最好的想象力来自不同文化视角的交汇。

04

Awareness · Connection · Creativity

觉察 · 连接 · 创造

We don't just throw people into a room and start a timer. Our methodology — built on self-awareness, deep human connection, and creative expression — creates a space where strangers become genuine collaborators in hours. That's why our alumni keep coming back.

和其他比赛不同，我们会在现场注入元创独有的方法论——以向内觉察为起点，以深度人际连接为纽带，以创造性表达为出口——能让陌生人在几小时内建立真正的信任与默契。这也是为什么我们的参与者总会再回来。
